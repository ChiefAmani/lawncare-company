# FINANCIAL_MODEL_INITIAL.md

## 1. Executive Summary
This initial financial model projects the path to $50k/month revenue, focusing on key unit economics and initial operational assumptions.

## 2. Key Assumptions

### Revenue Drivers
*   **Average Client Value (ARPU):** $200/month (Mowing + basic treatments, based on "Mow+" bundle and 12-month level billing).
*   **Target Clients:** 250 recurring clients to reach $50,000/month.
*   **Client Acquisition Rate:**
    *   Month 1-3: 30 clients/month (hyper-local print, Nextdoor, referrals)
    *   Month 4-6: 40 clients/month (expanded neighborhoods, Google LSA)
    *   Month 7-9: 50 clients/month (consistent marketing, strong referral loop)
*   **Churn Rate:** 3% monthly (Target from Optimization Recommendations).

### Cost Structure
*   **Variable Costs per Client (COGS):**
    *   Fuel: $10/client/month (estimated, based on route density)
    *   Supplies (blades, string, minor repairs): $5/client/month
    *   Labor (direct, per client): $50/client/month (estimated, 2-man crew efficiency)
    *   **Total Variable Cost:** $65/client/month
*   **Fixed Costs (Monthly):**
    *   Owner Salary: $5,000 (initial, scaling with growth)
    *   Marketing/Advertising: $1,000 (door hangers, yard signs, Google LSA)
    *   Software (CRM, scheduling): $150
    *   Insurance: $200
    *   Equipment Maintenance/Lease: $1,000 (initial, for stand-on mowers)
    *   Office/Admin: $300
    *   **Total Fixed Costs:** $7,650

## 3. Unit Economics
*   **MRR per client:** $200
*   **Gross Profit per client:** $200 (ARPU) - $65 (Variable Cost) = $135
*   **Gross Margin:** ($135 / $200) = 67.5%
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $50 (Target from Optimization Recommendations)
*   **LTV (Lifetime Value):** ARPU * Gross Margin % / Monthly Churn Rate = $200 * 0.675 / 0.03 = $4,500
*   **LTV:CAC Ratio:** $4,500 / $50 = 90:1 (Very healthy, indicates strong profitability per customer)
*   **Payback Period:** CAC / Monthly Gross Profit per Customer = $50 / $135 = 0.37 months (approx. 11 days)

## 4. Initial Monthly P&L Projection (Month 9, at 250 clients)

*   **Revenue:** 250 clients * $200/client = $50,000
*   **Variable Costs:** 250 clients * $65/client = $16,250
*   **Gross Profit:** $50,000 - $16,250 = $33,750
*   **Fixed Costs:** $7,650
*   **Net Income (before tax):** $33,750 - $7,650 = $26,100

## 5. Break-Even Analysis
*   **Break-Even Clients:** Fixed Costs / Gross Profit per Client = $7,650 / $135 = 56.67 clients
*   **Break-Even Revenue:** 56.67 clients * $200/client = $11,334

## 6. Initial Risks
*   **Execution Risk:** Achieving target client acquisition rate and maintaining low churn.
*   **Financial Risk:** Underestimating variable costs or fixed overheads.
*   **Market Risk:** Competitive pricing pressure impacting ARPU.
