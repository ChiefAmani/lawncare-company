# GROWTH_STRATEGY.md

## Overview
This document outlines the growth strategy for the lawncare company, focusing on market penetration, expansion, and customer acquisition.

## Key Growth Pillars
1. Market Penetration: Deepen presence in existing markets.
2. Market Expansion: Enter new geographic areas.
3. Service Diversification: Introduce new services.
4. Customer Retention: Enhance loyalty and reduce churn.

## Initiatives
- Targeted marketing campaigns
- Partnership development
- Digital presence optimization
- Referral programs
