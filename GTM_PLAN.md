# Go-To-Market (GTM) Plan: Scaling to $50k/Month

## 1. Executive Summary
**Objective:** Scale from <$5k/month (single neighborhood) to $50k/month.
**Core Strategy:** Hyper-local route density, aggressive referral loops, and transition to recurring revenue models.

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
*   **Demographics:** Homeowners in middle-to-upper-middle-class suburban neighborhoods.
*   **Psychographics:** Value time over money. Take pride in home appearance but lack the time, equipment, or desire to maintain it themselves.
*   **Behavioral:** Currently using inconsistent solo operators or doing it themselves inconsistently. Active on Nextdoor and local Facebook groups.

## 3. Acquisition Channels (The "Dominate the Neighborhood" Playbook)

### Channel 1: Hyper-Local Print (Door Hangers & Yard Signs)
*   **Tactic:** "5-Around" strategy. Every time a lawn is mowed, place door hangers on the 5 adjacent houses.
*   **Message:** "We just mowed your neighbor's lawn. We have capacity for 2 more homes on this street. Get your first mow for $19 when you sign up for a monthly plan."
*   **Yard Signs:** Place branded yard signs at corner lots of current customers (offer a $10 discount for keeping the sign up for 7 days).

### Channel 2: Digital Local Communities (Nextdoor & Facebook)
*   **Tactic:** Leverage existing customers for social proof.
*   **Execution:** Incentivize current customers in the single neighborhood to post a review on Nextdoor. 
*   **Offer:** "Mention [Customer Name] and get 50% off your first month."

### Channel 3: The "Neighbor Network" Referral Program
*   **Tactic:** Build a viral loop within subdivisions.
*   **Offer:** "Give a Free Mow, Get a Free Mow." When a customer refers a neighbor, both get a free service credit.
*   **Strategic Value:** Increases route density. Two houses next to each other take 1.5x the time of one house, but yield 2x the revenue.

## 4. The Funnel
1.  **Awareness:** Door hangers, yard signs, branded trucks/trailers.
2.  **Consideration:** Nextdoor reviews, Google Local Service Ads (LSA).
3.  **Conversion:** Frictionless online quoting (using tools like Jobber or Yardbook). No in-person estimates for basic mowing.
4.  **Retention:** Auto-pay credit card on file. No cash, no checks.

## 5. 30/60/90 Day Roadmap
*   **Day 1-30:** Saturate current neighborhood and 2 adjacent neighborhoods with door hangers. Set up Google Business Profile. Implement CRM (Jobber/Yardbook).
*   **Day 31-60:** Launch referral program to existing base. Begin Nextdoor organic posting. Transition all current clients to auto-pay.
*   **Day 61-90:** Expand to 5 new neighborhoods. Launch Google Local Service Ads. Hire first part-time helper to increase daily capacity.
