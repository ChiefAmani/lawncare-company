# MARKET_ANALYSIS.md

## Overview
This document provides an analysis of the current market landscape for lawncare services, identifying key trends, opportunities, and challenges.

## Market Size and Trends
- Growing demand for professional lawncare.
- Increasing adoption of eco-friendly practices.
- Seasonal fluctuations in demand.

## Target Demographics
- Homeowners (single-family, suburban)
- Commercial properties (HOAs, small businesses)

## Opportunities
- Untapped geographic areas.
- Demand for specialized services (e.g., organic lawn treatment).

## Challenges
- Intense competition.
- Labor shortages.
- Weather dependency.
