# PERFORMANCE_REPORT.md

## 1. Executive Summary
This report outlines the key performance indicators (KPIs) to be tracked for monitoring the financial health and operational efficiency of the lawncare business, aligned with the $50k/month scaling objective.

## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### A. Financial KPIs
1.  **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** Total predictable revenue from all active subscription clients each month.
    *   *Target:* $50,000/month by Month 9.
    *   *Frequency:* Monthly.
2.  **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** Total revenue divided by total active clients.
    *   *Target:* >$200/month.
    *   *Frequency:* Monthly.
3.  **Gross Margin:** (Total Revenue - COGS) / Total Revenue.
    *   *Target:* >65%.
    *   *Frequency:* Monthly.
4.  **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Total sales & marketing spend / New clients acquired.
    *   *Target:* <$50.
    *   *Frequency:* Monthly.
5.  **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** ARPU * Gross Margin % / Monthly Churn Rate.
    *   *Target:* >$4,000.
    *   *Frequency:* Quarterly.
6.  **LTV:CAC Ratio:** LTV divided by CAC.
    *   *Target:* >3:1 (ideally >5:1).
    *   *Frequency:* Quarterly.
7.  **Cash Runway:** Current cash balance / Monthly net burn rate.
    *   *Target:* Maintain >6 months of runway (once profitable, focus on growth capital).
    *   *Frequency:* Monthly.

### B. Operational KPIs
1.  **Client Churn Rate:** (Clients lost in month / Clients at start of month) * 100.
    *   *Target:* <3% monthly.
    *   *Frequency:* Monthly.
2.  **Route Density (Average Drive Time Between Jobs):** Average time spent driving between service locations.
    *   *Target:* <7 minutes.
    *   *Frequency:* Weekly (spot checks).
3.  **Crew Utilization Rate:** Billable hours / Total available crew hours.
    *   *Target:* 65-75%.
    *   *Frequency:* Weekly.
4.  **Upsell/Cross-sell Rate:** Number of clients purchasing additional services / Total clients.
    *   *Target:* 10% monthly increase in upsold clients.
    *   *Frequency:* Monthly.

## 3. Reporting Frequency & Format
*   **Weekly Snapshot:** Key operational KPIs (Churn, Route Density, Crew Utilization) to be reviewed by operations.
*   **Monthly Financial Review:** All financial KPIs, P&L, and Cash Flow statements to be reviewed by management.
*   **Quarterly Strategic Review:** LTV, LTV:CAC, and detailed scenario analysis to inform long-term strategy.
*   **Format:** Data to be presented in a structured JSON or CSV format for easy conversion to Excel dashboards.

## 4. Data Sources
*   CRM/Scheduling Software (e.g., Jobber, Yardbook)
*   Accounting Software (e.g., QuickBooks)
*   Marketing Analytics (e.g., Google Local Service Ads dashboard)
