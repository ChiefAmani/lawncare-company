# Service Expansion Plan: Increasing LTV and Margins

## 1. Strategic Context
Currently offering "Mowing Only." Mowing is a low-margin, high-frequency commodity service. It is the "loss leader" or acquisition tool. To reach $50k/month, we must increase Average Order Value (AOV) and Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) by cross-selling high-margin services to the captive mowing audience.

## 2. Phase 1: The "Mow+" Bundle (Immediate Implementation)
Stop selling "just mowing." Shift to a standardized basic package.
*   **Current:** Mowing ($40-$50/cut).
*   **New Standard:** Mowing, Edging, String Trimming, and Hardscape Blow-off.
*   **Pricing:** $55-$70/cut.
*   **Why:** Customers expect this anyway. Standardizing it justifies a higher base price and ensures a premium look that acts as a billboard for neighbors.

## 3. Phase 2: High-Margin Add-Ons (Next 3-6 Months)
Once trust is established via weekly mowing, upsell these services via email/text campaigns.

### A. Fertilization & Weed Control (The Profit Engine)
*   **Economics:** Extremely high margin (70%+). Requires licensing in most states, but chemical costs are low compared to labor.
*   **Strategy:** Offer a 6-step annual program. 
*   **Execution:** Send a text to all mowing clients in early Spring: "We noticed some crabgrass starting. Want us to add our 6-step weed control program to your account?"

### B. Seasonal Cleanups (Spring/Fall)
*   **Economics:** High ticket ($300-$800+ per job).
*   **Strategy:** Mandatory opt-out for existing clients. "We are scheduling Fall Leaf Cleanups for November. Reply 'SKIP' if you don't want this service, otherwise we will add you to the schedule for $X."

### C. Aeration & Overseeding
*   **Economics:** High hourly rate. Equipment can be rented initially.
*   **Strategy:** Run a campaign in late August/September. "Thick grass chokes out weeds. Book aeration today."

## 4. Phase 3: Project-Based Work (6-12 Months)
*   **Mulch Installation:** High markup on materials, fast execution.
*   **Shrub Trimming:** Bi-annual service.
*   **Winter Revenue:** Snow removal (if applicable to geography) to maintain cash flow during off-season.

## 5. The Value Equation Application
*   **Dream Outcome:** A pristine, country-club level lawn.
*   **Likelihood of Achievement:** 100% (we do it for them).
*   **Time Delay:** Zero (instant results after service).
*   **Effort/Sacrifice:** Zero (auto-pay, we show up automatically).
