# Ad Spend & Financial Recommendations

## 1. Unit Economics & Assumptions
Before allocating budget, we must define the financial guardrails.
*   **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** $200/month (assuming weekly/bi-weekly service + minor treatments).
*   **Gross Margin:** 40% (after labor, fuel, materials).
*   **Average Customer Lifespan:** 24 months.
*   **Lifetime Value (LTV):** $4,800 Revenue / $1,920 Gross Profit.
*   **Target Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $150 - $200. (This allows for a highly profitable 10:1 LTV:CAC ratio based on gross profit).

## 2. Monthly Budget Allocation (Example: $2,000/mo)

**A. Google Local Services Ads (GLSA) - 50% ($1,000)**
*   *Why:* Highest intent, lowest risk. You only pay for actual leads.
*   *Expected CPL (Cost Per Lead):* $25 - $40.
*   *Expected Leads:* 25 - 40 leads.
*   *Target Conversion:* 40% (10-16 new customers).

**B. Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Lead Ads - 30% ($600)**
*   *Why:* Proactive outreach to homeowners who aren't actively searching but have the pain point.
*   *Campaign Type:* "Instant Form" Lead Generation. Offer a specific hook (e.g., "First cut is $25 when you sign up for a monthly plan").
*   *Expected CPL:* $15 - $30.
*   *Expected Leads:* 20 - 40 leads.
*   *Target Conversion:* 20% (4-8 new customers).

**C. Nextdoor Neighborhood Sponsorships - 10% ($200)**
*   *Why:* Hyper-local brand awareness and route density.
*   *Strategy:* Sponsor the 2-3 zip codes where you already have the most customers to cluster your routes.

**D. Retargeting (Meta) - 10% ($200)**
*   *Why:* Conversion rate optimization.
*   *Strategy:* Target anyone who visited the website or engaged with FB/IG pages in the last 30 days but didn't submit a lead form. Show them customer testimonials and a strong Call to Action.

## 3. Scaling Triggers
*   **Green Light:** If blended CAC drops below $150 and route density allows for capacity, increase GLSA and Meta budgets by 20% month-over-month.
*   **Red Light:** If CAC exceeds $250, pause Meta top-of-funnel ads. Re-evaluate the offer and landing page conversion rate. Shift all budget to GLSA until unit economics stabilize.
