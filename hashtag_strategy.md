# Hashtag & Local SEO Strategy

To maximize organic reach, we will use a tiered hashtag strategy. Every Instagram and Facebook post must include a mix of these three tiers to balance broad discoverability with hyper-local intent.

## Tier 1: Hyper-Local & Geo-Targeted (40% of tags)
*These are the most critical tags. They have lower search volume but extremely high conversion intent.*
*   #[City]LawnCare
*   #[City]Landscaping
*   #[City]SmallBusiness
*   #[Neighborhood]Living
*   #[City]RealEstate (Targets realtors and new homeowners)
*   *Example:* #AustinLawnCare #TravisCountyLandscaping #DomainAustin

## Tier 2: Service-Specific (40% of tags)
*These categorize the exact service being performed, capturing users looking for specific solutions.*
*   #LawnMaintenance
*   #WeedControl
*   #LawnAeration
*   #SpringCleanup
*   #LawnEdging
*   #Mulching
*   #TurfManagement

## Tier 3: Broad Industry & Aesthetic (20% of tags)
*These have massive volume. They rarely drive direct local leads but help trigger the algorithm to categorize the account correctly and boost overall engagement.*
*   #LawnCareCommunity
*   #OddlySatisfying
*   #CurbAppeal
*   #LawnStripes
*   #LandscapingLife
*   #YardGoals

## Implementation Rules
1.  **Volume:** Use 15-20 hashtags per Instagram post (placed in the first comment or at the bottom of the caption). Use 3-5 hashtags per Facebook/LinkedIn post.
2.  **Rotation:** Do not copy-paste the exact same block of hashtags on every post. Rotate Tier 2 tags based on the specific image/video content to avoid shadowbanning.
3.  **Location Tagging:** Always use the native Location Tag feature on IG/FB in addition to hashtags. Pinpoint specific affluent neighborhoods rather than just the broad city name.
