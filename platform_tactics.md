# Platform Tactics & Go-To-Market Strategy

## 1. Google Local Services Ads (GLSA) - *Primary Acquisition Channel*
*   **Role:** Bottom-of-funnel lead capture. Captures high-intent users searching "lawn care near me".
*   **Tactic:** Become "Google Guaranteed". This is non-negotiable for local service businesses. You only pay per valid lead (phone call or message), not per click.
*   **Optimization:** Dispute unqualified leads immediately to protect budget. Solicit 5-star reviews aggressively, as GLSA ranking is heavily dependent on review velocity and rating.

## 2. Nextdoor - *Hyper-Local Penetration*
*   **Role:** Route density building and community trust.
*   **Tactic:** Do not just run standard ads. Use "Neighborhood Sponsorships" to dominate specific zip codes. 
*   **Organic Play:** Encourage existing happy customers to post organic recommendations. A single organic recommendation thread on Nextdoor can yield 5-10 new clients in the same neighborhood, drastically improving route density.

## 3. Facebook - *Retargeting & Community*
*   **Role:** Mid-funnel consideration and demographic targeting (Homeowners 35-65+).
*   **Tactic:** Run Lead Generation Ads offering a "Free Instant Estimate". 
*   **Ad Creative:** Use user-generated content (UGC) style videos. A selfie-style video of the owner saying, "Hey [City] homeowners, if you're tired of spending your weekends mowing..." outperforms highly polished corporate videos.
*   **Retargeting:** Pixel website visitors and retarget them with customer testimonial carousels.

## 4. Instagram - *Visual Proof & Brand Premium*
*   **Role:** Top-of-funnel awareness and justifying premium pricing.
*   **Tactic:** Focus heavily on Instagram Reels. The algorithm favors local content if tagged correctly.
*   **Content:** "Oddly satisfying" content works incredibly well here. Think straight-line edging, high-speed mowing time-lapses, and power washing driveways. Tag the specific neighborhood location in every post.

## 5. Direct Mail (Automated Integration)
*   **Role:** Hyper-targeted route density.
*   **Tactic:** "Five-Around" strategy. When a new client is booked, automatically trigger a direct mail postcard to the 5 closest neighbors saying, "We're taking care of your neighbor's lawn on [Street Name]. Want us to do yours while we're in the neighborhood?"
